Dead by Daylight
An asymetrical horror survival game which pits 4 survivors against 1 killer.
Killer
Abilities vary between killers, the goal is to hook all 4 survivors 3 times each.
Survivor
Survivor goal is to repair 5 generators and open the exit gates before being killed by the killer.
